CLUB MAHINDRA'S LIVING EARTH

Introduction

The concept was to create a transportable luxury habitat that can be set up at various locations as needed by Club Mahindra, minimizing environmental disruption by eliminating the need to construct structures repeatedly. Inspired by the form of a blooming flower, the pod design expands up to three times its original size, while the joinery details draw inspiration from the anatomy of Spider. The pod includes a toilet, pantry, storage facility, and a collapsible bed, offering complete functionality in a compact form. It is equipped with a retractable base covered by a stretchable solar fabric that provides essential shelter and harnesses solar energy to cater various requirements.
