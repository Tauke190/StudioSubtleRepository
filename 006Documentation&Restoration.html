PATAN RESIDENCE

The 100-year-old Patan Residence on Akhibahal Marg is part of a cluster of homes sharing a central communal courtyard.
It was documented for an adaptive reuse project, focusing on its structure and post-earthquake condition.

Lalitpur, Nepal

HIMAL HOME

Himal Home, a 70-year-old multi-storey house in Patan, Lalitpur, was proposed for adaptive reuse as a homestay.
The house was manually documented, and detailed drawings were created to capture its structure and essence.

Lalitpur,Nepal

AKHIBAHAL.

Akibahal, a 150-year-old house in Patan, Lalitpur, is organized around a central
courtyard with semi-public spaces and a horse stable on the ground floor.
Known for its ornate windows, the house was documented for its structure,
layout, post-earthquake condition, and window detailing.

Lalitpur,Nepal

PIMBAHAL LAKE.

The area, anchored by Pimbahal Lake, serves as a lively hub connected to narrow, mixed-use residences and
urrounded by eateries and shops. It was documented to study its activity and impact on local lifestyles.
