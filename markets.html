Narratives of Sirphu/January 7 , 2022

The Sirphu mountain has not just witnessed the age old story of Lord Shiva but also holds the memories of the union of villages and the coming together of people from villages near by. The Dohori songs sung, the rare sight of ox fighting, big and small stalls by people paused the silence of the mountains and its stream along with the folding in and out of the nearby land. This pocket of land served as a niche for people to witness the beauty of the land and exchange greetings. Today, it exists as a land that finds its mention in the stories of this historic place narrated by its young and its old

The studio adopts an approach towards a design that is people centric and context sensitive .
