XAVIER ACADEMY EXTENSION.

Introduction

The future school is envisioned as a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that goes beyond traditional classrooms, emphasizing experiential, collaborative, and conventional education. The establishment is seen as an aid that encourages the various possibilities of learning crucial for overall development while augmenting an environment that is collaborative, recognising the need to create a platform for student which is inclusive and indulgent. Thereby, through focus in aiding, encouraging and augmenting the true essence of learning, the school for tomorrow was carved to existence. Designed as a "window to the world," it nurtures curiosity and creativity by providing versatile spaces like corridors that blend into in-between areas that encourage informal learning. Innovative facilities, such as a Fab Lab, offer opportunities for hands-on experimentation, while the use of local crafts and sustainable materials, like Lokta paper as an element of design, fosters a connection to cultural roots. The design thoughtfully integrates vibrant student areas with serene spaces for educators, promoting well-being and productivity. Overall, the school cultivates a collaborative atmosphere that supports holistic development, innovation, and adaptability to future needs.
