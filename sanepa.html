SANEPA RESIDENCE

Introduction

The project involved the remodeling of a 20-year-old house for a family of four according to the principles of vaastu. The shift of the entry from the south side to the east side being one of them. Another key objective was to redesign the gathering and dining areas to create a sense of connect, allowing these spaces to blend seamlessly into one another. Landscape design played a significant role, with a water body enveloping the entrance and a variety of indoor and outdoor trees integrated into the design. he overall design was aimed to suit the family's current lifestyle while accommodating the needs of future generations.
